## How to test a system without running it {#2017-conf-heisenbug}

{% include notitle [testing_tag](../_includes/tags.md#testing) %}

The systems we develop are becoming more complex every day. And it seems there is no escape from the ubiquitous complexity that permeates everything. One aspect of this complexity is configuration. On the one hand, configuration greatly affects the stability and availability of the system; on the other hand, very little attention is paid to checking its correctness. In his talk, Andrey Satorin tells about how configurations are tested and how useful it is.

@ [YouTube](https://youtu.be/KaeEjsAjV6A)

This talk will be interesting to anyone who wants to learn a simple way to increase the stability and availability of a system in production.
